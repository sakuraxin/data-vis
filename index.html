About this Visualization

This visualization uses the Intercative SlideShow to tell the story of LIFE EXPECTANCY. Built on scenes, with annotations, various parameters and some triggers.

There are four charts in this visualization:

  • Line chart shows the world life expectancy from year 1960 to year 2016.
  • Bubbles charts show the average life expectancy of each area from year 2000 to year 2016 and the average life expectancy of each country during the same time period.
  • Bar chart shows the life expectancy of Japan and Sierra Leone from year 2000 to year 2016.

    About the dataset

    The dataset could be found through LIFE EXPECTANCY, named "life expectancy at birth, total.csv" which contains all the data of each country, area and world from year 1960 to 2016 (exclude 2017 since data of 2017 are not available). This visualization mainly talks about the world life expectancy from 1960 to 2016, average life expectancy of each country and area from year 2000 to 2016. From the analysis of average Life Expectancy of each country from year 2000 to year 2016, Japan has the largest average life expectany and Sierra Leone has the smallest life expectancy. The last scene with bar charts shows the life expectancy, sum and the difference of Japan & Sierra Leone from year 2000 to year 2016 (exclude 2017 since data of 2017 are not available). I used python to generate useful data file and filter data.

The last slide shows the sum and difference of life expectancy of Japan and Sierra Leone from year 2000 to year 2016.
    The last stacked bar chart shows the life expectancy of Japan and Sierra Leone from 2000 to 2016. The bottom green bars stand for life expectancy of Sierra Leone, the upper blue bars stand for Japan. Tooltip of blue bars (life expectancy of Japan) shows the sum of the two countries of a certain year, the country name of Japan, the life expectancy in the same year, the difference of life expectancy between two countries and year. Tooltip of green bars shows the country name of Sierra Leone, the life expectancy in the same year, the difference of life expectancy between two countries (the same absolute number of the difference shows in tooltip of Japan, but with opposite sign) and year.
